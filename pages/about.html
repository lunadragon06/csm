About us

Science for the population

Our commitment to make science accessible to the population

Over the course of human history, science has developed from our early understanding of fire, wind, water & earth to explore everything from galaxies far away to the very building blocks of life itself.

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us. We believe science should not be confined to textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. Many of our exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits - they only come to life at night when everyone's gone home).

For kids

Want to learn more about science? We run special holiday clubs and activites where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Our activities and events are made to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to explore.

For teachers

As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create learning & exploring. Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning labratory, and provide video presentations that will excite and inspire them.

For researhcers

Looking to get involved with our team of researchers & academics? Our museum offers ways for you to use our resources & contribute towards them. We have online records, laboratory space, and a working relationship with some number of universities around the country.
