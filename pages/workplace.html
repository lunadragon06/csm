Work with us?

Get involved

Be a volunteer

Volunteers use their time and effort to keep displays in good order and ready for visitors to come.

As a volunteer you will see behind the scenes of the museum, get access to the staff-only lectures, monthly lunch where all staff and volunteers discuss ideas for new exhibits & strategies for the museum. Contact us to find out how you can get involved.

Support us

There are various ways you can support the museum. Donations are welcome and an important way we keep museum open & accessible to the whole community.

Support us by donate items to museum's collections. If you have items or collection you think others will enjoy, let us know to & contact our Collections Department for assistance.

Be part of our internship

Want to work in a museum? Enjoy all fun & excitement of sharing the wonders of nature with people?

You could be the person to enjoy an internship at the museum. You'll be learning from academics and people passionate about science and sharing it with the wider community.
