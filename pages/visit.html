Location

The museum is located at Mellomila 56, 7018 in Trondheim town

Opening hours

The museum is open every day all year around, except on mondays

Admission

The entrance is free for all visitors. Guided tours arranged every hour

Accessibility

Our museum has wheelchair ramps, audio guides and braille display signs

Cafeteria

The Café is located 1st floor and provides light food, soft drinks, cofee, snacks and more

Souvenir shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia, as well as great gifts and activity packs
